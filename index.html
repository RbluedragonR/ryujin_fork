How to Buy RyuJin (RYU) on Uniswap
1. Create a Wallet

If you don't have one yet, start by creating a MetaMask, TrustWallet, or any preferred wallet. In this guide, we will use MetaMask, which you can set up on either a desktop computer or an iOS or Android mobile device. MetaMask allows you to buy, sell, send, and receive $RYU. Ensure to keep your recovery phrase in a safe place.

2. Get Some Ethereum (ETH)

If your wallet doesn’t have ETH, you can purchase it directly through MetaMask, transfer it from another wallet, or buy it on a centralized exchange and then withdraw it to your wallet.

3. Go to Uniswap

After you have Ethereum in your MetaMask wallet, you need to connect your wallet to Uniswap. To do this, visit the app.uniswap.org website, click 'Connect to a wallet,' and select MetaMask.

4. Swap ETH for $RYU

Choose the amount you wish to trade and set your slippage to 0.5-1%. Click Swap and confirm the transaction. When your wallet prompts you, confirm the action.
